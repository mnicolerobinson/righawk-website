How It Works

From scan to booked.
Automatically.

RigHawkAI runs on autopilot so you can stay focused on the road — not the load board.

🔍
You Set Your Preferences
Tell RigHawkAI your equipment type, preferred lanes, minimum rate per mile, and quiet hours. Takes two minutes during onboarding.
🎯
AI Discovers & Scores Loads
Every 10 minutes, the engine scans available loads and scores each one out of 100 based on equipment match, rate per mile, mileage, and your lane preferences.
📞
Ravyn Calls You
When a load scores 85 or above, Ravyn calls your phone and reads you the full details — origin, destination, rate, mileage, weight, broker, and pickup date — clearly and naturally.
✅
You Accept or Pass
Just say "book it" or "pass." Ravyn logs your decision, marks the load accordingly, and keeps scanning. Your dashboard updates in real time.

FAQ

Questions drivers
actually ask.

No fluff. Straight answers about how RigHawkAI works and what to expect.

How does Ravyn know which loads to call me about?
+
Ravyn uses a scoring engine that evaluates every available load across four dimensions: equipment match (does it fit your truck?), rate per mile (how it compares to market average), mileage (optimized for your preferred range), and lane preference (bonus points for routes you've told us you like). Only loads scoring 85 or above trigger a call — so when your phone rings, it's always a legitimate opportunity worth your attention.
What if I don't want calls late at night or early in the morning?
+
Available on Growth and Fleet plans, you can set custom quiet hours directly in the app — for example, 10 PM to 7 AM. Ravyn will never call during those hours, period. You can also flip on a Do Not Disturb toggle at any time to pause calls instantly, then turn it back off when you're ready to roll again. Your sleep is protected.
Will Ravyn keep calling me even if I already have a load?
+
No. Ravyn checks whether you have an active booked load before every call. If you're already on a run, she won't call — she knows you're busy. The moment your load is marked complete, she's back on the hunt. This is one of several built-in guardrails designed to make sure every call she makes is relevant and timely.
What if I miss Ravyn's call?
+
No problem. The load stays visible in your Loads screen inside the app, where you can review it, see the full details, and book it yourself. You'll also receive a push notification alongside the call so you always have a record. Ravyn won't call back about the same load, but if a similar load appears on a different lane, she'll call about that one separately.
How is RigHawkAI different from DAT or a load board?
+
Load boards like DAT require you to log in, search manually, scroll through dozens of listings, and negotiate yourself — every single time. RigHawkAI does all of that for you automatically. The AI scans continuously, scores every load before you see it, filters out anything that doesn't match your equipment or RPM requirements, and then has Ravyn call your phone when something good appears. You don't lift a finger until a great load is already in front of you.

Can I upgrade or change my plan later?
+
Yes, anytime. You can upgrade from Starter to Growth or Growth to Fleet at any point from your profile settings. Upgrades are prorated — you only pay the difference for the remaining days in your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date so you keep your current features until then.
What equipment types does RigHawkAI support?
+
Currently supported: Dry Van, Flatbed, Refrigerated (Reefer), Car Hauler, and Step Deck. Car Hauler drivers can also specify their vehicle capacity during onboarding so Ravyn only surfaces loads that match how many cars you can move. More equipment types are being added — if yours isn't listed, let us know at signup and we'll prioritize it.
I have more than one truck. Which plan do I need?
+
The Fleet plan at $999/month supports up to 20 trucks. Each driver gets their own Ravyn configuration — separate equipment type, preferred lanes, quiet hours, and call phone number. You get a fleet-wide analytics dashboard so you can track earnings, load acceptance rates, and average RPM across your entire operation in one place. It's the full dispatcher replacement for growing carriers.
Is my data and personal information secure?
+
Yes. RigHawkAI is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with encrypted data storage, secure authentication, and role-based access controls. Your phone number, earnings data, documents, and personal information are never sold or shared with third parties. Your call history and load data are private to your account only.
